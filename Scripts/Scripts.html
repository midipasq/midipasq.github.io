Macaulay2 Code
Macaulay2 scripts for most research projects are collected under the Research tab.

  • M2Computations - A script for verifying Proposition 2.7 in "Associated Primes of Spline Complexes" (arXiv:1410.6894) for the polytopal complex in Example 2.8.
  • ShellNonFree - A script for computing the module of piecewise continuous polynomial functions on the cone over two embeddings of a shellable polytopal complex in the plane. One of these has the structure of a free module over the polynomial ring in three variables while the other does not.
  • AlgebraicSplines.m2 - Source code for a package for working with algebraic splines on polyhedral complexes. Joint with Gwyneth Whieldon, Eliana Duarte, and Daniel Bernstein.
