Hyperplane arrangements and deletion-restriction methods for splines


Recommended Reading
  • The algebra of splines: duality, group actions, and homology . Survey article on splines and the recommended reading for our focus group.
Other articles we may reference
  • Generalized Splines on Arbitrary Graphs . In this article the setup for generalized splines on graphs -- which we plan to use -- was first explained.
  • Module Bases for Multivariate Splines . A generalization of Saito's criterion from hyperplane arrangements to the context of splines.
  • The Euler multiplicity and addition-deletion theorems for multiarrangements. It would be great to find addition-deletion theorems for splines that mirror what has been done for multiarrangements.
Working notes
Below you will find some preliminary notes on some of the topics that are either directly related to the working group, or are tangentially relevant.
  • A restriction map and applications to freeness, with emphasis on hyperplane arrangements . This writeup has a rough draft of the restriction map, as well as proposed problems.
  • Notes on presenting the non-trivial homology module for splines on fans in three dimensions . This writeup is technical. The main point is to prove that the module of continuous splines on the chambers of a three-dimensional central hyperplane arrangement is free if and only if the hyperplane arrangement is '3-generated.' This gives a starting point if the goal is to inductively construct free spline modules.
